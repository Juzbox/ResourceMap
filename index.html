Delhi Resource Repository

Welcome! Please find here a regularly updated list of groups seeking donations for those affected by the communal riots in NE Delhi. Scroll down to see how you can help.

How can I get involved?

Donate Items

+ You can see the support your money is providing and ensure items purchased are of quality and new.

– It can be difficult for organisations to manage, be it storage, delivery, and may or may not be what is most relevant at the time of donation.

Donate Money

+ Gives the group you are donating to the chance to spend it on what is most relevant be it gas for the delivery truck or items they are low on.

– You will have to trust the organisation as they may not always be able to give you proof of how your money is used since they are already busy doing hard work!

Volunteer

+ Have a direct impact on the lives of those you are helping.

– Not a one time engagement, you need to be committed and able to volunteer multiple times to build rapport with those you are working and those you are helping.

About

We are a collective of designers hoping to help with the tools we have handy. The details provided on here are updated to the best of our knowledge from the limited information we have access to (mainly via Twitter and Instagram) and we are therefore open to any comments / feedback / concerns you may have about it.

If you are one of the organisations listed on this site, we must have reached out to you in advance to make sure you were comfortable with us sharing this information but please let us know if you wish anything removed or added. You will notice that we are not including any contact numbers or exact addresses, only social media handles, for that purpose.

This site was designed as open source as possible so others can contribute to the effort of making this site relevant to those referring to it and is therefore a work in progress we hope to improve over the coming months.
